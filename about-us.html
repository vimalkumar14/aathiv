Our Firm “Aathiv” is established in 2004 as Aathiv Arts and Crafts in Theni, Due to our creative and innovative appeal Our firm saw a steady growth in terms of its client base, We utilized every opportunities and niches into successful business, that led to our firm to explore our multiple branches under the name of “Aathiv Suruki and Gravels” established in 2012 , “Aathiv Builds and Designs” established in 2015 and “Aathiv Recuva Grits” established in 2018 . Aathiv is the collaborative effort of a team of over 50 craftsmans ,designers,engineers and labours whose enthusiasm and creativity has resulted in our accomplishments

Aathiv Builds & Designs


Aathiv Builds and Designs is a Indian based design firm established in 2016 ,with a commitment to making your daily places to be more lively. We believe that each and every one of our design matters, and that through our work, we can make a positive difference in the world. We strive to create buildings that are not just things in themselves, but serve as catalysts for positive urban and social change. We provide Architectural & Interior and graphic design services and also provides Project Management service for both Private clients and Corporates. We also appreciate the collaborative nature of design and consider it crucial to incorporate the varying requirements of the clients, consultants, contractors and other associated personnel involved in the project. On the whole we believe in creating a holistic environment which celebrates life.

Aathiv recuva grits


Our company Aathiv Recuva Grits is the first company in tamilnadu to make utilization of Construction wastes and turn it in to Reusable construction materials since 2018, The best way to recycle construction waste is by using it in new projects, Sticking to this core concept we have done our research and came up with Our two construction efficient recycled products which would be R- Sand and RE-BLOCKS, Based on the test reports our products ensures all the material quality guidelines and are ready to act as a part of alternates for traditional construction materials, Recycling construction materials provides an opportunity to achieve cost savings. These savings include the cost of new materials, and also transportation and disposal costs.

Aathiv suruki and gravels


We are the pioneers in supplying readymade Brick Bats known as Suruki all over tamilnadu from 2011 , Brick bats and Lime are the major construction materials required during the execution of weathering Coarse, By preferring those materials ready made it does save a lot of construction time and labour cost in days, We do also supply all kinds construction materials at competitive prices. We procure all the construction materials directly from the manufacturers through strategic partnerships and supply to our clients at the lowest prices possible.
